    Admission Procedure

  • No cut off marks.
  • Admission will be strictly on merit basis.
  • No form will be received after last date of submission at any situation.
  • Admission form must be filled up in all respects. (Last attended examination, Mark Sheets & Certificate. Gap Certificate if you needed, four copies of stamp size photograph)
  • Parents/Guardians must attend the time of admission.
  • Original documents must be shown at the time of admission.
  • The decision of the authority of the academy will be the final and supreme.

    • Concession for students


  • Admission fees will be reduced according to the marks obtained in the HSLC examination.

    • Hostel Fees Structure


  • Section IX & X, H.S. Arts, Commerce and Science.
  • Admission fee Per Annum 10,000.00.
  • Mess Dues (Per Month): Due to discussion of committee .

    • Transportation Fee Structure


  • Enrol fee Rs.4,800.00 (Non Refundable).
  • Per Month fee for the distance between 0 to 5 K.M. Rs.1000.00
  • Per Month fee for the distance between 6 to 10 K.M. Rs. 1500.00
  • Per Month fee for the distance between 10 to 15 K.M Rs. 2000.00
  • Per Month fee for the distance between 16 to 20 K.M Rs. 2200.00

    • Rules and Regulations for Transport Communication


  • Registration fee for transportation is Rs. 4800/- only. The amount will be deducted/collected Rs. 400/- only per month for 12 months.
  • A student can get up or get off from the vehicle only at the spot specified by the authority of the Academy.
  • The monthly transportation fees have to be paid in first week of every month.

    • Hostel Rules and Regulations


    Academy will provide hostel facilities for both Boys & Girls separately at minimum fees.


  • Students desirous to reside in the hostel should apply to the authority at the time of submission.
  • Hostel admission will be finalized at the beginning of the session.
  • Boarders will not be allowed to go outside after 6.30 P.M. & to come out before 8 A.M. from the hostel without permission of the warden.
  • For one boarder Academy will provide with a bed, a table, a bulb/tube light. Other items like bed-sheets, pillow, quilt etc will have to procure by the boarder themselves. They will have to
  • repair/replace tube lights and socks whenever necessary.
  • Boarders are allowed to enjoy light & fans They cannot use electric heater, iron, mobile phone, radio, tape recorder etc.
  • Academy will provide the boarders meals three times a day at morning, noon and at night. Red tea will be supplied at morning and evening. They will be supplied meat at dinner on Wednesday & Friday, eggs on Sunday & Monday, fish on Saturday & Tuesday.
  • Act of entering Girl’s to Boy’s and Boy’s to Girl’s hostel is strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking, drinking wine, chewing alcoholic substances are strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the authority will be considered the Final and Supreme.

    • Rules and Payment Fees


  • Tuition fee must be paid for 12 months (May to June) and tuition fee must be paid in case or online before 12th of each month.
  • Admission fee must be paid at the time of admission.

    • Library Facility


  • Library is the best friend of the students when they are at leisure. Eden valley Academy Offers a well equipped library with text book, magazines, news papers and books of different tastes. There is the reading room attached with the library.
