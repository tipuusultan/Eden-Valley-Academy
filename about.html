FROM THE CHAIRMAN'S DESK

SUCCESS DOESN'T COME TO YOU... YOU GO TO IT - MORVO COLLINS

EVA, a co-educational institute at private sector was established on 16th March, 2012 at agro-based village Chamata, the Prime Centre of Paschim Nalbari area that occupies a special position in the history of education, culture, art and socio-economic development of Nalbari District of Assam. EVA is forerunner for the development of the youth resources of the greater west Nalbari area. The strength of the Academy lies in its sincere attempts and strong determination to help the students in achieving goals. The EVA provides you all sorts of facilities for your all round development, so that you can grow up as a proud citizen of our motherland. Therefore if you have a dream, Then to it with us and make it a reality. The EVA provides three specific branches of formal education as follow.

1. Lower Primary Section
2. Upper Primary Section (Class VI to Class VIII)
3. High school Section (IX to X)
4. Higher Secondary Section (Commerce, Arts, Science)

Mission


Eden Valley Academy (EVA) to build the foundation for
an individual to achieve academic excellence, professional
competence and to develop personal and social skill with the touch of real human worth’s. The MISSION of the Academy is to produce awareness and curiousness for higher education among the rural and mostly economically backward people.

Motto


“Gyanang Bibhati BIddya” is the motto of the academy which means knowledge is acquired by education.

Goal


“The Academy is to make efforts for all round development of students through academic pursuit and co-curricular activities, imparting value based education so that the students keep pace with the changing needs of our society.
