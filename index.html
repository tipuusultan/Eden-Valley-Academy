Mriganaka Pritam Bhagwati
Co-Ordinator's Voice

As a coordinator, I believe that every child is born with a unique potential and he/she only requires help to accomplish it. According to Gyani purush a person who gives happiness to those who have given him happiness and he does not hurt those who have caused him suffering. Practicing this kind of behavior is truly human.  The law of nature says that when you give away your share to others, your needs will be looked after by nature. If you live this life for the benefit of others, you will not incur any loss or face obstacles in your life, if you live this life for the benefit others. All your wishes will be fulfilled.
In order to make a child extraordinary, parents visualized on the following points:


(1) Please co-operate with the Academy in ensuring an effective education for your child. This can be done by
(a) Providing facilities of students at home,
(b) Providing textbooks, notebooks and other requirements for your child,
(c) Assisting & guiding in doing homework,
(d) Assisting your child in removing any deficiency in a particular subject.
(2) Should you feel or notice that your child is not making the desired progress. Kindly meet the Principal/Co-ordinator. You are always welcome the Academy to ensure the progress of your child.
(3) You may contact the subject & class teacher in the staff room.

"Honours and rewards fall to those who show their good qualities in action" - Aristotle​

In the present age, we are living amidst full of competition, challenges & agitation.
Modern technology and the scientific method has redefined our lifestyles and formed a new world which has been contracted into a global village. With powerful changes taking place in almost every walk of life the society requires able leaders, technocrats, social reformers, educationist in the real sense who can lead our society and prepare people to embrace them. Provides of educational services should, therefore, bring out rural human resources, competent of model concept, and converting them into action. Success, we believe is not a matter of luck and genius. But DISCIPLINE and HARD WORK are the wings which help us to fly towards our goal. There is no substitute for hard work. The EVA connects the three points of education - the parents, the students, and the college. That will help you to become a better person.  In the process of globalization of education, EVA will take the role of challenge for developing competent for the future changeable global society.
